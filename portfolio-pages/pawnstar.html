Project details

In the fascinating world of artificial intelligence and chess, we've embarked on an exhilarating journey to train a chess-playing AI that rivals grandmasters. The project combines the power of reinforcement learning with sophisticated training strategies to create an AI chess player that continuously improves its skill set.
